DeTanks

Play to Earn Game on Solana Blockchain.

DeTanks is a play-to-earn NFT tank battle game where players can participate in PvP battles, mint NFT tanks with unique attributes, stake NFTs and make profit while enjoying the game.

DeTanks offers many earning opportunities that most NFT projects lack. We prioritize our community values.
